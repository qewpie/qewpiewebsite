CHILDS PLAY COLLECTION

Bringing back childhood nostalgia and excitement into adult franchises! Comfort your inner child while also giving in to your adult interests! Created in Adobe Photoshop & Illustrator. Prints are available for $20 - Satin or Matte Available.

GROOVY MYSTERY

Album “Issues” by Korn. Wanted to create a unique & psychedelic design that contrasted their branding. Focused heavily on typography and image placement. Played around with 3D effects and word movement.

LOVE, SOSA

Album “Finally Rich” by Chief Keef. Wanted to something subversive to his branding & distinctiveness. Focused on typography & image placement. Played around with various Adobe features, getting familiar with the program.

I LOVE THE SMITHS

Album “The Queen is Dead” by The Smiths. Wanted to create something playful and child-like, in an attempt to subvert the band’s actual message. Focused on typography and image placement.

MEET MY FRIENDS

"South Park Theme" by Primus for the show South Park. Wanted to create a drawing that one of the kids in the show would make. Played with paper texture and extereme typographic styling. Focused on typography.

MISTKI VINYL

Be The Cowboy by Mistki Typographic Vinyl Design. No images, just type! Created in Adobe InDesign & Illustrator.
