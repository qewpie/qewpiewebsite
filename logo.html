DEATH GRIND

Business Mission Statement: GRIND TIL YOU DROP DEAD! Grinding the freshest, highest-quality, and unique coffee beans until we die. Let us serve you till then.

CLICK ON LOGO TO FOLLOW MY PROCESS!

Design Problem: Design a brand identity & product mock-ups. Fictional Business created for GDES 3352: Identity & Symbols. Death Grind is an alternative cafe that caters to emo, goth, metalheads, and anybody who lives a weird life or just wants.

Solution: Hand drawn logo & font. Darker color palette for an edgy vibe; green, red, and a dark purple. Sharp shapes, inspired from metal band fonts.

D SPA

Client work for small Vietnamese run nail salon in Minnesota.

Design Problem: Client wanted a fresh new look while also encompassing older elements; stronger brand identity, cohesive color palette & recognizable logo. Focused on legibility and accessibility due to older clientele.

Solution: Hand Drawn logo. Used a lighter color palette; green, pink, and yellow from older branding. Employed a sleek and elegant font, paired with a simpler body front for comfortable reading. Airy and open space design.
